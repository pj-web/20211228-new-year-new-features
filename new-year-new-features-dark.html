As we head into the New Year, let’s take a moment to remember the basics: never trade or invest blindly, do your research, and always be patient. In our final newsletter of 2021, we share some of the most popular new features we launched over the last 12 months.

Introducing Quick Search
Quick Search makes finding drawing tools and chart functions a breeze. Type in a keyword and then select the tool you need. For example, try searching “Trend” or “Price” to see all the tools and functions related to these two words.
 
Learn Quick Search
 
 
 
Desktop 1.0 Has Arrived
Our Desktop app works for Mac, Windows, and Linux. It has all the functionality of our web product, but also includes added features like tab management and more.
 
Get it here
 
 
 
Pine Script’s Epic Year
This year, we rolled out more improvements to Pine than ever, among them Pine Script v5, interactive inputs and tables. And finally, our thriving community of script authors has published 10,000+ new, free Community Scripts during the year.
 
See every Pine update
 
 
 
Spark Your Research
We continue to improve Watchlists so you’re always informed about your favorite symbols. We launched Sparks, Timelines & Snaps, and the ability to share your Watchlist with friends.
 
Try Sparks
 
 
 
More Brokers, More Data
Connect your TradingView account to your favorite brokerage and trade from the chart. We partnered with 10+ new brokerages including Saxo, easyMarkets, Oanda, Tiger, Tickmill, and Bitstamp. We also added new data that spans DeFi, DEXs, economics, and more.
